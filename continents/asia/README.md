## Overview

Asia holds the highest concentration of sacred sites on Earth, encompassing the birthplaces and primary centers of Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, Sikhism, Taoism, Confucianism, Shinto, Zoroastrianism, and Islam. From the towering Himalayas to tropical temple complexes, from desert monasteries to island shrines, Asia's sacred geography is vast and profound.

## Special Considerations

### Permits & Access
Many Himalayan and Tibetan sites require special permits. Sacred sites in contested regions may have access restrictions.

### Cultural Sensitivity
Dress codes, footwear removal, photography restrictions, menstruation taboos, and dietary requirements vary by tradition and location.

### Best Seasons
- Himalayan sites: May-June, September-October
- Desert regions: October-March
- Monsoon regions: November-February
- Equatorial regions: Year-round with rainy season considerations
