## Overview

North America's sacred geography extends from Arctic ice to desert canyons, encompassing tens of thousands of years of indigenous spiritual traditions. Medicine wheels, burial mounds, rock art sites, and vision quest locations honor the First Nations' deep connection to land. Colonial-era missions and contemporary spiritual centers add layers to this rich sacred landscape.
