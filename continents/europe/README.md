## Special Considerations

### Access & Timing
- Mount Athos: Men only, permits required
- Many sites closed Sundays for worship
- Orthodox monasteries: Modest dress, head coverings for women
- Private chapels: Appointments needed

### Seasonal Beauty
- Spring: Wildflowers in megalithic sites
- Summer: Long northern light hours
- Autumn: Pilgrimage season, harvest festivals
- Winter: Christmas markets, solemn atmosphere
