# ⛰️ South America - Condor's Flight Path

*Incan temples, Amazonian power, and the spine of the Andes*

## Overview

South America's sacred landscape rises from Amazon jungle depths to Andean peaks, encompassing ancient civilizations, living indigenous traditions, and Catholic syncretism. From Machu Picchu to Easter Island, from sacred waterfalls to desert geoglyphs, the continent pulses with spiritual energy both ancient and contemporary.

## Major Regions

### Andean Nations

#### Peru
- **[Cusco Region](peru/cusco/)** - Machu Picchu, Sacsayhuamán, Sacred Valley, Ollantaytambo
- **[Lake Titicaca](peru/titicaca/)** - Floating islands, Taquile, birthplace of Inca civilization
- **[Northern Peru](peru/north/)** - Kuélap, Chavín de Huántar, Sipán, Caral
- **[Southern Peru](peru/south/)** - Nazca Lines, Colca Canyon, desert geoglyphs
- **[Amazon Region](peru/amazon/)** - Iquitos area, ayahuasca centers, jungle power spots

#### Bolivia
- **[La Paz & Surroundings](bolivia/la_paz/)** - Valley of the Moon, Tiwanaku, death road shrines

- **[Eastern Bolivia](bolivia/east/)** - Jesuit missions, Amboró sacred sites

#### Ecuador

#### Colombia
- **[Bogotá Region](colombia/bogota/)** - Gold Museum, Zipaquirá salt cathedral

## Indigenous Traditions

### Andean
Quechua, Aymara traditions with Pachamama (Earth Mother) reverence, mountain worship (apus), coca ceremonies

### Amazonian
Hundreds of nations: Shipibo, Asháninka, Yanomami, Kayapó—each with unique cosmologies, plant medicine traditions, and sacred geographies

### Patagonian
Mapuche, Tehuelche, and other southern nations with sacred mountain and water traditions

## Special Considerations

### Altitude
Many Andean sites above 3,000m require acclimatization. Coca tea helps. Acute mountain sickness is serious.

### Access & Permits
- Machu Picchu: Advanced booking required
- Amazon sites: Guide mandatory
- Some indigenous territories: Permission needed
- Protected areas: Entry permits

### Climate & Seasons
- Andes: Dry season May-September
- Amazon: Year-round, but less rain June-October
- Patagonia: October-March (summer)
- Atacama: Year-round, cold nights

### Plant Medicine Tourism
Ayahuasca centers require careful vetting. Work only with traditional, ethical practitioners.
